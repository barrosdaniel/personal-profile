Personal Information

When I'm not bringing design ideas into life, I like to read about technology or watch science documentaries or sports.

After approximately 20 years working in Human Resources, I have switched careers into IT working with Information Systems. I’m now pursuing Bachelor Degree in Information Technology, attempting to concentrate further on Computer Science subjects. Apart from English, I also speak Portuguese (native) and can speak some Spanish, which has many similarities to Portuguese.
